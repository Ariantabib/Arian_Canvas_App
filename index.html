How to Use
1

Load Reference Image

Tap the Image button to upload a drawing reference from your gallery.

2

Position Over Canvas

Point your camera at your drawing surface. The overlay will appear on screen.

3

Adjust Perspective

Tap Corners to show the 4 corner handles. Drag each corner to match your canvas edges in the camera view.

4

Fine-tune Opacity

Use the opacity slider to blend the overlay so you can see both the reference and your drawing.

5

Choose Lens / Zoom

Use the Lens button to pick different zoom levels. On Galaxy S23 Ultra, try 3x for detailed work.

6

Lock & Draw

Lock corners when positioned, use Hide UI for a clean view, then trace your drawing!
